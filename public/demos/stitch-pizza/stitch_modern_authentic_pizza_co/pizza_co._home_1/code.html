Our Story

It began in a small Naples courtyard with a family secret and a stone oven. Today, Pizza Co. continues that legacy by sourcing only the finest DOP ingredients.

Our dough rests for 48 hours, creating a light, airy crust that carries the authentic flavor of wood smoke and tradition. We treat this as our guiding principle, translating the spirit of Italian dining with every location we open.
